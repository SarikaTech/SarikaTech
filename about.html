Sals Shipping Pvt Ltd is a privated limited company formed in 2008 with headquarters in Navi Mumbai, India. We are committed to providing outstanding ship management services, putting the quality of our service and the safety of our people at the heart of everything we do.

OUR WAY AHEAD
OUR VISION

To become the preferred ship manager & maritime service provider for a selected group of clients.

OUR MISSION

To achieve our vision, we will use the best maritime practices & approach the jobs with passion & commitment.

VALUES WE FOLLOW
HEALTH & SAFETY

At SALS our safety policy supports our commitment to zero injuries and the highest standards of protecting property, equipment and the environment complying with the ISM code.

OUR GOAL

At SALS our goal is to provide reliable and efficient management. We keep our promises and deliver our very best across the spectrum of ship management services every single time.

SECURITY

Our priority is always to prevent unforeseen incidents, protecting the interests of our Owners, Clients and Seafarers and ensuring that their reputations remain intact and financially sustainable.

POLICY

We comply with all applicable international, national, state and local laws and regulations. We take action to control the risk of any possible personal injury, damage to the environment, property and equipment.

ECO-FRIENDLY

At SALS we are committed to a policy of conserving the environment, adopting the latest environmental standards and going beyond what is required by regulations and legislation.
